Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal financial information that you voluntarily enter into the application. This includes expense transactions, categories, descriptions, amounts, and dates. We also collect account information such as your email address and authentication credentials. No financial account numbers or bank credentials are stored in our system.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking functionality of Spendly. We use your entered transactions to generate spending summaries, category breakdowns, and time-based reports. This information helps you understand your financial habits and make informed decisions about your spending.

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into Spendly is stored in a secure SQLite database with foreign key constraints enabled. Your financial information is kept private and is only accessible through your authenticated account. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or share your personal financial data with any third parties. We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior within the application. The only external services used are for hosting and database storage, which are bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Access and Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can view, edit, or delete any expense entry at any time through the application. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems. We do not retain backups of deleted user data.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking scripts. Your browsing behavior within the application is not monitored or recorded for advertising purposes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related inquiries in a timely manner.
